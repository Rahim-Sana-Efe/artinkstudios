ArtInk Studios - Music Covers FAQ

1. What is a music cover?

A music cover is when you perform a song originally made by another artist. We help you create your own unique version!

2. Can I release my cover song?

Yes! But you need a license to do it legally. We'll guide you where to get it.

3. Do I need to credit the original artist?

Yes, always credit the original artist in your post or video.

4. Can I post my cover on YouTube or TikTok?

Yes! But the platform may share some revenue with the original creators.

5. Can I change the song?

You can change the style or feel, but not the lyrics without extra permission.

6. Do you help with video?

Yes! We can help you record a video for your cover if you need it.

7. Can I do any genre?

Absolutely! Pop, rock, rap, indie, acoustic, you name it.

8. Do you provide backing tracks?

Yes! We can help you find or create the perfect backing music.

9. How do I book a session?

Message us on our site or social media in contact us page!
